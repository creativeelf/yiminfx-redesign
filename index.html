    BIO

    Yimin is a Technical Artist who specializes in making tools, shaders, and real-time effects. He is currently working at Meta, helping to bring metaverse experience to Meta's social media apps through augmented reality. Previously, he has worked on multiple VR experiences for a Transformer VR center, and on films such as Marvel's Ant-Man and The Wasp: Quantumania. Through his diverse work experiences across games, films, and tech, he has developed a robust generalist skill set while leveraging specialized learnings from each industry.

    Yimin also loves venturing in an ever-more emerging intersection of art and technology, always staying up to date with the latest breakthroughs in generative AIs and Large Language Models. Outside of work, he enjoys making fun prototypes with new tech and exploring new places with a camera in hand.
